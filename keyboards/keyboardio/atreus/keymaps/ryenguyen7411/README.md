## BASE LAYOUT

.-----------------------------,           .-----------------------------.
|  Q  |  W  |  E  |  R  |  T  |           |  Y  |  U  |  I  |  O  |  P  |
|-----------------------------|           |-----------------------------|
|  A  |  S  |  D  |  F  |  G  |           |  H  |  J  |  K  |  L  | FN4 |
|-----------------------------------------------------------------------|
|Shift|  Z  |  X  |  C  |  V  |VI11 |MOUSE|  B  |  N  |  M  | FN2 |Ctrl |
|-----------------------------------------------------------------------|
|Ctrl |     | Alt | Cmd | FN2 | FN2 | FN1 | FN3 |  ◀  |  ▼  |  ▲  |  ▶  |
'-----------------------------------------------------------------------'

- Shift Tap = ESC
- Right Ctrl Tap = ESC
- FN1 Tap = Space
- FN2 Tap = Space
- FN3 Tap = Space
- FN4 Tap = Enter
- MOUSE Tap = FN1 on
    - Shift + MOUSE = FN1 off
    - Ctrl + MOUSE = FN1 off
