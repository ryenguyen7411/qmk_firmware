## BASE LAYOUT
* ,-----------------------------------------------------------------------,
* |  ~  |  Q  |  W  |  E  |  R  |  T  |  Y  |  U  |  I  |  O  |  P  | <-- |
* |-----------------------------------------------------------------------|
* |  FN1  |  A  |  S  |  D  |  F  |  G  |  H  |  J  |  K  |  L  |   FN2   |
* |-----------------------------------------------------------------------|
* |  Shift    |  Z  |  X  |  C  |  V  |  B  |  N  |  M  |  .  |     |RCmd |
* |-----------------------------------------------------------------------|
* | Ctrl| Alt | Cmd |      FN3       |     FN4    |  ◀  |  ▼  |  ▲  |  ▶  |
* '-----------------------------------------------------------------------'

- FN1 Tap = ESC, Double Tap = Toggle FN1
- FN2 Tap = Enter
- FN3 Tap = Space
- FN4 Tap = Space

* |     |     |VI12 |VI11 |     |     |     |     |S_05 |     |     | Del |

- S_01 = Ctrl + ◀
- S_02 = Ctrl + ▶
